INVESTMENT PHILOSOPHY

The Eolos Liquid Equity Long/Short is a low net exposure strategy that endeavors to:

  • Provide attractive stand-alone returns
  • Protect during equity market declines
  • Deliver a long volatility return profile

The proprietary alpha engine emphasizes stocks that are in transition higher or lower and via the analysis of “on” and “off” exchange transactional data in combination with qualitative and proprietary data, implements a hedged equity strategy with an investment horizon of days to weeks.
